# Acknowledgements

## W3C Specifications

This XQuery/XPath engine implements specifications developed by the
[World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)](https://www.w3.org/) and the
[Qt4 Community Group](https://qt4cg.org/):

- [XQuery 3.1](https://www.w3.org/TR/xquery-31/) (W3C Recommendation)
- [XQuery 4.0](https://qt4cg.org/specifications/xquery-40/xquery-40.html) (Community Group Draft)
- [XPath 3.1](https://www.w3.org/TR/xpath-31/) (W3C Recommendation)
- [XPath 4.0](https://qt4cg.org/specifications/xpath-functions-40/Overview.html) (Community Group Draft)
- [XQuery and XPath Data Model 3.1](https://www.w3.org/TR/xpath-datamodel-31/) (W3C Recommendation)
- [XPath and XQuery Functions and Operators 4.0](https://qt4cg.org/specifications/xpath-functions-40/Overview.html) (Community Group Draft)
- [XQuery Update Facility 3.0](https://www.w3.org/TR/xquery-update-30/) (W3C Recommendation)
- [XQuery and XPath Full Text 3.0](https://www.w3.org/TR/xpath-full-text-30/) (W3C Recommendation)

These specifications represent years of collaborative work by the W3C XSL Working Group,
the W3C XML Query Working Group, and the Qt4 Community Group. We thank all editors and
contributors.
